Penant Graph

Pennant is a bullish continuation chart pattern that forms after a strong price movement. It is characterized by converging trendlines that form a small symmetrical triangle or flag shape. Pennants typically indicate a brief consolidation or pause in the market before the price trend resumes. A breakout from the pennant pattern usually occurs in the direction of the preceding trend, accompanied by increased trading volume. Confirmation of the pattern is typically sought through a sustained price movement in the direction of the breakout, but false breakouts can occur, so risk management and additional technical or fundamental analysis are important.
