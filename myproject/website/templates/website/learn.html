  • Pengraph

    Pennant is a continuation pattern after a flagpole, signaling a potential trend continuation.

  • Flag Graph

    Flag patterns on stock charts signal potential trend continuations, with a period of consolidation after a price move.

  • Wedge Graph

    Wedge patterns on stock charts signal potential trend reversals, with narrowing price movements and increasing volatility.

  • Ascending Triangle Graph

    Bullish chart pattern with upward sloping resistance and horizontal support, indicating potential price breakout?

  • Descending Triangle Graph

    Bearish chart pattern with downward sloping support and horizontal resistance, indicating potential price breakdown.

  • Symmetrical Triangle Graph

    Neutral chart pattern with converging trend lines, indicating potential price breakout in either direction.

  • Cup and Handle Graph

    Bullish chart pattern with a rounded cup and a handle, indicating potential price continuation.

  • Head N Shoulders Graph

    Bearish chart pattern with a peak (head) and two lower peaks (shoulders), indicating potential price reversal.

  • Double Top and Bottom Graph

    Bullish (double bottom) or bearish (double top) chart pattern with two price highs or lows, indicating potential price reversal.

  • Gaps Graph

    A gap occurs when the stock's price jumps over one or more trading periods, indicating potential momentum or trend change.
